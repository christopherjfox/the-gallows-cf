Welcome to my "The Gallows" Game


Objective:

The Gallows is a hangman game, with goal of the game being to figure out the hidden word

The Rules are simple.

1: The player will be allowed 8 wrong attempts to guess the hidden word.

2: The player must enter in a letter in the text box and hit the submit button.

4: Each wrong guess will cause the turn counter in the top right hand corner of the screen to increase by one and advance the image of a player being hung by a nuse

3: The player is given one hint that is related to the secret word

5: If the letter exist already the player will be notified and not be penilized a turn

6: If the player guesses a correct letter the leter will appear on the hidden word

7: The player will win the game if all the hidden letters are guess with out running out of turns

8: The player will have an option to start a new game after a game is won or lost
